Hi, I am Huiqi Meng.

An apprentice in Research, Writing, and Education.

I am an MA student in Applied Linguistics at Teachers College, Columbia University. I am drawn to the beauty of natural language, the richness of cultures, and the profound depth of human thought.
I am interested in semantics, particularly in Degree Semantics, Quantification, and Model-theoretic Semantics. I am also exploring NLP applications, including areas such as chatbots and digital humans.

Moreover, I am a member of the Linguistic Society of America (LSA).
